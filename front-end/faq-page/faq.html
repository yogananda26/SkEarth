Our Privacy Practices

It's important that you read our full statement or notice to understand what information we hold, how we may use it, and what your right are, but if you don't have time to read it all now, here is a quick summary:

  • We collect information about our customers and users of our services along with our employees, franchisees, development agents and other third parties. The information collected is either personal information (such as your contact information) or non-personal (such as webpages accessed).

Frequently Asked Questions

How to be a sizzle member?

+

Sign up by filling out the registration form. This may involve providing your name, email address, phone number, and other personal details. Read and agree to the terms and conditions of the loyalty program.

How do I order via Online?

+

Select the items you want to order and add them to your cart. Make sure to check the quantities and any special instructions you may have for your order. When you have finished adding items to your cart, proceed to the checkout page. Here you will be asked to provide your delivery or pickup information, payment details, and any additional instructions. Double-check all the information you have provided and confirm your order

How we do delivery?

+

Delivery is an essential part of the food industry, especially during these unprecedented times. To ensure that our customers can enjoy their favorite cuisine in the comfort of their own homes, we offer delivery services.

How to customize my order?

+

You can give special instructions to customize your order. You can add it in notes before you checkout

Where do I find Sizzle's outlet?

+

You can use the outlet menu to search for the nearest outlet location to your current or desired location.

Can I make a reservation?

+

You can make a reservation over the phone, or in-person at the restaurant.
