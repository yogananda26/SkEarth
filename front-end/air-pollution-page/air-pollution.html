Guide Air Quality Index (AQI)

Good (LEVEL 1)

Fresh air, deep breaths, outdoor fun (healthy for everyone).

Fair (LEVEL 2)

Hint of haze, sensitive beware, shorten outdoor time (caution for sensitive groups).

Moderate (LEVEL 3)

Cough in the wind, masks advised, limit activities (everyone impacted, stay indoors if feasible).

Poor (LEVEL 4)

Stinging air, danger lurks, stay inside, protect lungs (hazardous for everyone, avoid outdoors).

Very Poor (LEVEL 5)

Suffocating fog, health at risk, seal windows, air purifiers (emergency situation, stay indoors).
